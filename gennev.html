Project Overview

Menopause Assessment Results Page

When I started working at Gennev, menopause awareness month was approaching, and they knew there would be an influx of traffic with the potential of being new patients. Their biggest source of traffic comes through their menopause assessment. With the big month coming up, focus shifted to the menopause assessment, it’s current performance.

Although a huge driver of traffic, the menopause assessment consistently delivered a low conversion rate. Gennev had done little to no user research on the menopause assessment since it launched, so they did not know where to start!

After collecting feedback from several women in menopause, the main issue boiled down to the assessment results page. I redesigned the layout, the content, and implemented accessibility improvements.
