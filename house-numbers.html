Project Overview

New product research & design - reverse mortgages

The company realized a large demographic of homeowners was being overlooked - senior homeowners. To fill this gap, House Numbers decided they wanted to offer reverse mortgages.

Reverse mortgage definition: A mortgage loan that allows senior homeowners to convert home equity into cash income.

Reverse mortgages do not have a good reputation - even though it can be an excellent tool! House Numbers wanted to change that.

I worked on designing a reverse mortgage landing page, loan estimate calculator and results page.

Pain Point #1

Lenders aren't straightforward with the loan requirements & logistics

Ideas for Solutions

  • Provide straightforward info on logistics and loan requirements
  • Placement of important info on landing page - must be easy to find
  • Provide testimonials on bottom on landing page /throughout pages to build trust

Pain Point #2

Lenders aren’t providing enough educational resources

Ideas for Solutions

  • Provide free educational resources - calculator and information guides, etc.
  • Use language focused on educating
  • Information need to be accessible from several parts of the website

Reverse Mortgage Calculator Iteration

Addressing the pain points

Final design of landing page, estimate calculator & results page

Requirements & loan logistics are easy to find

To build trust with the user, the most important information is front and center on the landing page. The light orange color used for the icons was selected to attract attention, while comortfing the user.

Free, easy to access educational resources provided

To build user confidence, I designed a reverse mortgage calculator that provides an instant estimate - no need to talk to a lender to learn on how much you qualify for.

Content and language is focused on educating vs. selling

To build trust with the users, I focused on creating content that educates the user and pulls them in.

Testimonials provided, acting as proof

To build trust with the user and to build confidence in House Numbers, testimonials are sprinkled throughout the site to act as proof that House Numbers can deliver on their promises.

Takeaways & Highlights

  • Empathizing is one of the the best tools to use to create a good user experience
  • Color can be a powerful tool in influencing user emotions and grabbing attention
  • Open space and small amounts of text increases readability for senior users
  • Contact info needs to be easy to find and provided in multiple places, especially for senior users
  • Senior users are more likely to navigate a page easily if it has little detail and fewer distractions
